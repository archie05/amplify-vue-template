About NCBA Internet Banking

Our internet banking product is a secure, fully transactional, internet-based banking platform that enables you to transact with us wherever and whenever it suits you. All you need is a stable internet connection.

Protect yourself from online fraud

As you transact online, it is important to be aware of internet fraud. Please note that NCBA will NEVER send you an email asking you to enter, reconfirm or change your security details or other personal information. If you receive such an email or if you believe you may have disclosed your details in any way, please call us immediately on +254 20 2884000 or email contact@ncbagroup.com

For more info on how NCBA protects you from online fraud, visit www.ncbagroup.com
